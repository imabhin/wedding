First meet

May 18 2020

To be honest, at first when it was just upto the stage of phone calls we didn't expect much, although it was fun. But then the most pleasant thing happend, He came all way long from Ranchi to Jsr, just to ask out for a 'Tea Date'. The first sight of hers was a relief for me and beautiful indeed, Bcz till this time many thoughts were rushing into my mind, 'Is she gonna come to meet me!, Does she looks exactly like her pictures?, what if it turns out to be some kind of a prank on me!'. To me, His first glimps was peeping from the cabs window, his eyes searching for me and he looked way too excited while me on the other side was way too nervous. A social guy and an intovert girl, could this be a match? had no idea until now...
